WHAT IS THE ROLE OF VALUES, EMPATHY AND SELF-AWARENESS IN LEARNING AND PROGRAMMING?

I think that the main purpose of empathy and self-awareness is to learn how to manage the inner critic and how we critique others.

Empathy is so important because it helps us to communicate effectively and solve problems in a productive manner. If I understand another person’s perspective then firstly, this person is more inclined to listen to me and secondly, I’m more inclined to listen to them in order to reach an endpoint. It’s a win/win scenario.

Self-awareness is equally important because it means we can learn how to reflect on our actual situation rather than assuming a negative standpoint. This enables us to assess what next steps we need to take in order to better ourselves. Who doesn’t want that?
